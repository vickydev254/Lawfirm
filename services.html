Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial consultation free?
Yes. Your first consultation with LexFirm is completely free and confidential. We believe you deserve to understand your options before committing.
How long will my case take?
Case timelines vary by complexity. We provide a realistic assessment during your consultation and keep you informed at every stage.
Is everything I say confidential?
Absolutely. Attorney-client privilege protects all communications. Your privacy is our legal and ethical obligation — no exceptions.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. We understand legal fees can be significant. Flexible payment arrangements are available and discussed transparently before we begin.
What should I bring to my first meeting?
Any relevant documents, contracts, correspondence, or court papers related to your matter. The more context you provide, the better we can advise you.
Do you handle cases outside New York?
Our primary jurisdiction is New York, but we collaborate with trusted partner attorneys in other states for federal and multi-jurisdiction matters.
